# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive community for everyone participating in this project.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and assume good intent.
- Focus criticism on ideas and implementations, not people.
- Protect private information and security-sensitive details.
- Help maintain a welcoming environment for contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, doxxing, and deliberate disruption are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns through a GitHub issue requesting private contact. Maintainers may remove content, reject contributions, or limit participation when needed to protect the community.
